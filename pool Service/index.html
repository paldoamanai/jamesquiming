Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our gentle pool care services

How often should I have my pool professionally cleaned?

For optimal results, we recommend professional cleaning every 4-6 weeks during peak swimming season. However, the frequency depends on usage, environment, and your pool's specific needs. Our team can assess your pool and recommend a customized maintenance schedule.

Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. We use only biodegradable, non-toxic cleaning solutions that are certified safe for families and pets. Our products meet the highest environmental standards while being equally effective as traditional chemicals.

What makes your cleaning method "gentle"?

Our gentle approach combines specialized equipment with careful techniques to avoid surface damage. We use soft-bristled tools, low-pressure cleaning systems, and pH-balanced solutions that clean effectively without being abrasive or harsh on your pool's materials.

Do you service saltwater pools?

Yes, we're experts in all pool types including saltwater systems. In fact, our gentle cleaning methods are particularly beneficial for saltwater pools as they help prevent corrosion and scaling issues common with these systems.

What areas do you serve?

We currently service the greater Phoenix metropolitan area including Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Tempe, and surrounding communities. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.
